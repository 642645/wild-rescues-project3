About Wild Rescues

Wild Rescues is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping injured, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. Our mission is to rescue animals in need, provide rehabilitation and medical care, and release them back into their natural habitats.

What We Do

  • Rescue injured and orphaned animals
  • Provide rehabilitation and medical support
  • Release animals back into the wild
